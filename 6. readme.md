# CCB-Induced Peripheral Edema Calculator

An interactive, evidence-based calculator to estimate the probability of calcium channel blocker (CCB)-induced peripheral edema in hypertensive patients.

## Methodological Background
This tool applies the multivariable logistic regression parameters derived from the multicenter trial:
* **Citation:** Tolla K.S., Solela G., Wondafrash G., et al. (2026). *Incidence and risk factors of calcium channel blocker–related edema in hypertensive patients: A Multicenter retrospective cohort study.* PLOS One.
* **Key Independent Predictor Included:** Prolonged daily standing duration ($\ge3$ hours) significantly increases risk (Adjusted Odds Ratio = 1.92).
